# Community

Whether you need help, want to share what you built, or want to contribute,
here is where to go.

## Support and Help

- **[Community Forum](https://community.pantavisor.io)** — ask questions, share knowledge, get help from the community
- **[GitHub Issues](https://github.com/pantavisor/pantavisor/issues)** — report bugs and request features

## Stay Updated

- **[GitHub](https://github.com/pantavisor)** — follow development and contribute to projects
- **[Twitter/X](https://twitter.com/pantavisor)** — latest news and updates
- **[LinkedIn](https://linkedin.com/company/pantacor)** — professional updates and company news

## Share Your Projects

Post your projects, tutorials, and success stories on the
[Community Forum](https://community.pantavisor.io), or tag us on social media —
IoT deployments, custom container configurations, integration examples, and
guides are all welcome. Outstanding projects may be featured in the official
documentation, case studies, and community highlights.

## Contribute

- **[Main Repository](https://github.com/pantavisor/pantavisor)** — core Pantavisor development
- **[Documentation](https://github.com/pantavisor/docs.pantavisor)** — help improve these docs

When contributing: be respectful and constructive, follow the project's
contribution guidelines, and report issues with clear reproduction details.
